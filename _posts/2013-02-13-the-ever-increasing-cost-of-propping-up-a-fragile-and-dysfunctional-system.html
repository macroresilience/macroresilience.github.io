Many of the posts on this blog focus on the stability-resilience tradeoff in complex systems which essentially means that preventing all disturbances eventually causes a catastrophic disturbance. Correspondingly, engineering small disturbances i.e. controlled burns can prevent catastrophic disturbances. ---
